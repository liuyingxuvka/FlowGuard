## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Process strategy selection has one development-process commitment
The Behavior Commitment Ledger SHALL register one `development_process` commitment for selecting a bounded outcome-equivalent process strategy, assign the executable strategy child model as primary owner, and connect DPF/API/agent guidance through typed relations without merging product or agent-operation ownership.

#### Scenario: Public DPF guidance references strategy selection
- **WHEN** DPF skill guidance promises strategy comparison
- **THEN** its source surface maps to the single strategy commitment and not to a duplicate commitment
